"The Golden Age of Wireless" was the first Thomas Dolby official record, and his best one indeed.

In my opinion this one, closely followed by Soft Cell's "Non-Stop Erotic Cabaret" are the most great records of what one time was called "SynthPop" or "TechnoPop" in the early 80's. Two gems to any eclectical listener.

Here are all the tracks that were included in the definitive reedition of "The Golden Age of Wireless". I supose that somebody has ripped them from a CD.

Nontheless, I reordered them and added another track called "The Wreck Of The Fairchild" that was included in the very first release of the vynil. I ripped and processed it ... think I've done a good job ;-)

The new order mirrors the original one. If you are used to the new releases, you will notice that in the original order there were smooth transitions between some songs that were lost in the reedition.

Finally, I include two winamp playlists for both "versions"
