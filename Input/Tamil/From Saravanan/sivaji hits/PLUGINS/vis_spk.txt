REQUIRED:

1)  Winamp v1.6 beta or newer
2)  Put vis_spk.dll (plugin) in your winamp directory

INFORMATION:

Four Modules

1) Digital
	
	This module is called digital because it displays the volume level
in 7 steps. The closer to the max it gets, the closer to red the colour gets.
	
2) Analog

	This module is similar to digital, except it displays the volume
level in an 'analog' form, (sortof), without any steps (very small ones).
	
3) Woofer and Tweeter

	This module displays the upper and lower halfs of the frequency
spectrum separately.  The lower half is displayed in the lower circle,
and the upper half inthe upper circle.

4) Glow

	This module is very CPU intensive, and not very optimised, and 
may well have some bugs, but I wanted to include it anyways!

CONFIGURATION:

Config can be entered from the plugin popup menu (right click), or through the
winamp plugin preferences 'configure' button.

Falloff Rate - how quickly the VU Meter falls back to zero with no sound
Amplitude - how sensitive the VU meter is, or how high it gets
Calculation Method - choose between Logaritmic or Linear, Linear requires amplitude to be quite high.
